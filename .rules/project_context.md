# Project Context & Tone

## Vision
An autonomous esports news platform (Web, TikTok, YouTube) managed by AI agents.

## Tone of Voice
- **Passionate**: We love esports.
- **Expert**: Deep knowledge of the game mechanics and scene.
- **Direct**: No fluff, get to the point.
- **"Insider"**: Use industry terminology correctly (gank, inting, smurf, etc.).

## Content Guidelines
- **Target Audience**: Gen Z & Millennials, Competitive Gamers.
- **Focus**: LoL, CS2, Valorant, Rocket League.
- **Formats**: 
  - News Articles (Facts + Analysis)
  - Short-form Video Scripts (Hook + Highlights + CTA)
  - Long-form Recaps (Structured, Narrative)
